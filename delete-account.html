1. How to Request Account Deletion

You can request the deletion of your YolkFlock account and all associated data at any time by sending an email to our support team.

Send your request to yolkflock@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete Account". Please include the email address associated with your account.

Send Deletion Request

2. What Gets Deleted

Upon receiving your request, we will permanently delete the following data within 30 days:

  • Your account profile (name, email address)
  • All farm data including chicken records and egg production entries
  • Tasks and notes created within the App
  • Notification preferences and settings
  • Authentication data linked to your account

3. What May Be Retained

Anonymized and aggregated usage data (not linked to your identity) may be retained for analytical purposes after account deletion.

We may also retain certain data if required by law or to resolve ongoing disputes.

4. Third-Party Services

YolkFlock uses Google Firebase for authentication and storage. Deleting your account with us will remove your data from our systems, but you may also want to:

  • Revoke YolkFlock's access in your Google Account settings if you signed in with Google
  • Review Google's data retention policies at policies.google.com/privacy

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about the account deletion process, feel free to reach out:
